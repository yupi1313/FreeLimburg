The Edge

Why Juana

In a rapidly evolving esports betting landscape, Juana Bischoff stands apart. Here's what makes her one of the most effective business development leaders in the industry.

01

The Full Stack BD Leader

Most BD professionals come from either sales or partnerships. Juana bridges both — she's held roles spanning Head of Sales, VP Sales, Director of Global Accounts, and now Market Lead. This 360° perspective means she understands every lever in the revenue chain, from cold outreach to strategic alliance management.

02

Deep Ecosystem Knowledge

Having worked at Bayes Esports (data layer), Sportradar (infrastructure layer), and Oddin.gg (product layer), Juana has lived across the entire esports betting value chain. She doesn't just sell — she understands the product from data ingestion to odds delivery, which makes her a trusted advisor, not just a vendor.

03

Market Pioneer in LATAM

While competitors focus on mature European markets, Juana went where the growth is — Latin America. She's not just selling into LATAM; she's building the market. From Brazil's regulatory awakening to the diverse esports preferences across Spanish-speaking countries, she's shaping how the region approaches esports betting.

04

Thought Leader, Not Just a Seller

Juana doesn't wait for leads — she creates demand. Through the "Expert to Expert" video series, keynotes at ICE Barcelona and GILA Elite, and regular media commentary, she's educating entire markets on why esports betting matters. When operators think esports, they think of Juana.

05

Relationship-First Approach

In her own words: "Understanding client culture and business needs is fundamental." In an industry full of transactional sales, Juana's consultative approach — understanding each operator's unique DNA before proposing solutions — creates partnerships that last years, not quarters.

06

15+ Years of Consistent Results

From her early BD career through Bayes Esports to her current role driving Oddin.gg's LATAM expansion, Juana has a proven, repeatable track record of scaling revenue, opening new markets, and building teams. That consistency across multiple companies and markets is rare — and it speaks louder than any title.
